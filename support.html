FAQ

What is franca?

franca is a voice-personalized language learning app. It uses your voice to generate speech in the language you're learning, so you can hear how phrases sound in your own voice.

Which languages are supported?

franca currently supports Spanish, French, Italian, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and Russian, with more languages coming soon.

Is voice enrollment required?

No. Voice enrollment is entirely optional. You can use franca with system-generated voices without providing any voice recordings.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete your voice profile or all app data at any time through the Settings menu in the app. See our Privacy Policy for details.
